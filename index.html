About Us

Your Partner in Cleanliness and Safety

At Karsen N Welch Chemicals Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to promoting hygiene and cleanliness across diverse environments. Our highly skilled team specializes in manufacturing and distributing premium-quality cleaning and hygiene chemicals, ensuring the highest standards of effectiveness and safety.

Our Mission
  • To deliver high-quality, reliable products that ensure cleanliness and safety in medical, industrial, and home settings.
What we Offer
  • Disinfectant Cleaning Chemicals: Guarantees top-notch sterility and performance for medical environments.
  • Household/Industrial Cleaning Solutions: Offers versatile and effective cleaning for a fresh, safe home.
  • Size Available: All products are available in liters or kilograms, with options for 1, 5, 10, and customizable sizes.
