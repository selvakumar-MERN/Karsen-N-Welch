Kitchen Care Products

Home Kitchen Care Products DishAura

Description: DishAura Dish Wash Liquid is a powerful grease-cutting formula designed to remove tough food stains, oil, and grime while being gentle on hands. Its high-foaming action ensures a thorough clean, leaving dishes, glassware, and utensils sparkling with a refreshing lemon fragrance.
Direction for Use: Mix 3–4 ml of Dish Wash Liquid in a small bowl with about 40 ml of water. Dip a sponge or scrub pad into the solution and wash dishes thoroughly. For Heavy Grease & Stains: Apply directly onto the sponge or dish, scrub gently, and rinse with clean water.
Handling: For external use only.
Do not mix with other products. Avoid contact with skin and eyes. Do not ingest.
First Aid: In case of accidental contact, rinse with plenty of water several times. Get medical attention if needed.
Storage: Keep out of reach of children. Keep container tightly closed.
Ingredients: Sodium Lauryl Sulfate (SLS) (28%), COCO Amido Propyl Betaine, Sodium Chloride, Triclosan, Perfume, Ethylenediaminetetraacetic Acid (EDIA), Demineralized Water (DMH2O), Citric Acid, Sodium Bicarbonate, Polyethylene Clycol (PEG).
